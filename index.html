Delete your Aerial Atlas account

This page lets you request deletion of your Aerial Atlas account and associated synced data.

If you still have access to the app, the fastest option is to delete your account directly in the app from Account → Delete account.

Request deletion by email View privacy policy

What will be deleted

  • Your Aerial Atlas account
  • Your synced custom moves
  • Your synced Flows
  • Your synced favorites and move progress

How to request deletion if you cannot access the app

Send an email to appsindevelopment@gmail.com from the same Google account you used to sign in to Aerial Atlas, or include that email address in your message.

Subject line: Aerial Atlas account deletion request

We may contact you briefly to verify the request if needed.

What happens next

After we confirm the request, we will delete your account and associated synced data within a reasonable time.

If any data must be retained for legal, security, fraud prevention, or regulatory reasons, that will be handled as described in the privacy policy.
