Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Aerial Atlas collects, uses, stores, and deletes information when you use the app.

Who we are

Aerial Atlas is an app for saving and organizing aerial moves, progress, and Flows

Contact: appsindevelopment@gmail.com

Information we collect

When you use Aerial Atlas with Google sign-in, we may process:

  • Your sign-in email address and account identifier
  • Your synced custom moves
  • Your synced Flows
  • Your synced favorites and move progress

Built-in move content that comes with the app is part of the app itself and is not personal data.

How we use your information

  • To sign you in and keep your account available across devices
  • To sync your saved content between devices when you are signed in
  • To maintain and improve the app
  • To respond to support or account deletion requests

How your information is stored

Aerial Atlas stores some information locally on your device. When you are signed in, synced account data may also be stored in cloud infrastructure used to operate the app.

Account deletion

You can request deletion of your Aerial Atlas account and associated synced data in either of these ways:

  • Inside the app: Account → Delete account
  • Outside the app: use the deletion instructions on the account deletion page

When your deletion request is processed, your account and associated synced data are permanently deleted within a reasonable time.

Some limited information may be retained where necessary for legal, security, fraud prevention, or regulatory reasons.

Sharing

We do not sell your personal data.

Data may be processed by service providers that help operate the app, such as authentication, hosting, and database providers, only as needed to provide the service.

Your choices

  • You can use parts of the app without signing in
  • You can sign out at any time
  • You can request account deletion as described above

Contact

For privacy questions or deletion requests, contact: appsindevelopment@gmail.com
